How to Juggle a Soccer Ball

From Zero to Pro - Step by Step

Step 1: The Drop & Kick

Hold the ball at chest height. Drop it, let it bounce once, and kick it back up to your hands with your laces. Repeat until you can do it 10 times without moving your feet.

Step 2: No More Bounce

Drop the ball, but this time, kick it back to your hands before it hits the ground. Keep your ankle locked and your toes pointed slightly up.

Step 3: The Alternating Feet

Try to get two touches: Left foot -> Right foot -> Catch. This builds the coordination needed for long streaks.

Pro Tip: Don't kick the ball too high! Keep it around waist height to maintain control. remeber to juggle as long as possible.
